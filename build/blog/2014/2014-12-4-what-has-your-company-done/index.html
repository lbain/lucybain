What has your company done to make you feel welcome?

At RoRO last month we discussed women in tech issues. It came with the associated minefield, and we navigated it as best we could. But one question came out of it that I found particularly interesting:

As a woman in tech, what is the best thing your company has done to make you feel welcome?

Hmmm.

My coworkers have always listened my suggestions and taken them seriously. When they are valid, we have adopted them. But, more importantly, when they aren't good suggestions they have explained why and had a discussion (not a lecture) around it. They have never dismissed my ideas with a wave of their hand, leaving me to wonder if the idea was bad, or just the messenger. I have learned so much from my coworkers, and I appreciate all the time they've invested in me.

How about you? What has your company done that's made you feel welcome?
